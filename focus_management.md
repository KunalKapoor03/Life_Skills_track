# Learning Process – Assignment

## Question 1

### What is Deep Work?
Deep Work means focusing completely on one task without any distractions. It helps us do better work in less time and improves learning, creativity, and productivity.

---

## Question 2

### According to the author, how can deep work be done properly?

1. Work in a quiet place with no distractions.
2. Set fixed time for focused work.
3. Do important tasks first.
4. Take small breaks to rest the mind.
5. Reduce activities like social media, emails, and useless meetings.

---

## Question 3

### How can you use these ideas in daily life?

1. Plan 2–3 deep work sessions every day.
2. Turn off phone and notifications while working.
3. Make a to-do list for important and less important tasks.
4. Fix start and end time for each work session.
5. Check your progress every week.

---

## Question 4

### What are the dangers of social media (in short)?

1. It reduces focus and attention.
2. It wastes time and can be addictive.
3. It increases stress and comparison with others.
4. It makes it hard to do serious and meaningful work.
5. It makes the brain want constant entertainment.
