## Question 3

**What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ZhoeSaPF-k)?**

Internal Locus of Control is the belief that we are responsible for the the things that happen in our life, including both successes and failures. It makes us believe that factors of the outcome are in our control, and this makes us put more effort and make us think that anything is achievable, since we have control over how much work we put into something.
